### Hi! I'm Brian.

I'm a 4th year PhD candidate at Stanford University working in the Stanford Computational Imaging Lab, advised by Prof. Gordon Wetzstein. I'm grateful to be supported by the NSF GRFP and the Stanford Graduate Fellowship.

My research focuses on developing physics-grounded neural rendering algorithms that unify computer graphics, physics, and ML, to enable new capabilities in next-generation AR/VR displays, 3D scene reconstruction, and virtual world generation. Currently, I am expanding this expertise into genertive world modeling, specifically in dynamic and consistent world generation.
