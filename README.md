### Hi! I'm Brian.

I'm a 4th year PhD candidate at Stanford University working in the Stanford Computational Imaging Lab, advised by Prof. Gordon Wetzstein. I'm grateful to be supported by the NSF GRFP and the Stanford Graduate Fellowship.

My work draws on first principles in vision and graphics to develop efficient visual world representations across image, video, and 3D domains, including token-efficient generation and adaptive tokenization, novel 3D representations, and fast rendering algorithms.
